3 Dog Breeds for First Time Owners

Name Description
Papillon Papillons are very happy and affectionate dogs. They are great for first time owners, as well as for those looking for a family dog.
Shih Tzu The dogs that fall under this breed are known to be friendly as well, and lively. Shih Tzus are great recommendations for anyone looking for a cute and sweet dog to take in.
Soft Coated Wheaten Terrier Soft Coated Wheaten Terrier's are quite bigger compared to the previous mentioned dogs, but are medium-sized. They too are happy and are great family pets as they are known to get along with children.
